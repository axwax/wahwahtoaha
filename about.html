About WahWahToAha

WahWahToAha.com will help you gain practice listening and speaking a language. It will take you from hearing a foreign language as meaningless ('Wah Wah') to meaningful ('Aha!').

Repetition and exposure is how to get fluency in a language. 10 thousand repetitions of a word? That should do it! But it will only work if you hear the word in different contexts, and are actively listening and trying to understand the phrases. WahWahToAha will reward repeated exercise attempts much more than running new exercises. It will also give different context to words, by generating sentences where possible. Numbers are the easiest way for us to generate random but grammatically correct sentences, but we'll add other, more interesting, ways as we go along.

Learning is also accelerated by trying to create sentences and phrases. So we're trying to have as many spoken question/answer exercises as we can, and not so much on the typing. We'll see how that goes!

Technology

We make heavy use of the voice recognition and speech synthesis APIs that are part of the HTML5 specification. Currently, Google Chrome is the only browser to fully support both aspects.
